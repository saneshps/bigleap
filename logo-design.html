Logo Design

Logos are the voice of the brand and the simplest form to identify your brand. We build the best logos using logo marks, typographic solutions, or a combination of both. Design a logo that your ideal customers will recognize at the first sight. Logo design is the best method to signify your business using branding and marketing tools.

Name and Logo are considered the most recognizable elements of any brand. Name and Logo of a brand complete and support each other and can perfectly express who we are and what we do. BigLeap’s main moto is to design a logo that can clearly and authentically communicate your brand to the people you are targeting to reach.

The logo is more than a simple sketch and a splash of color, it should be created dynamically to catch the audience's attention and should perfectly represent the business. Creating a logo is a crucial part of branding which has to be done carefully and creatively that involves multiple stages. If you wish to build a unique and memorable brand then you have reached the correct destination.

The logo design service package will include Design, Logo Reasoning, Logo Suitability Check, and Visual Language. Product tags and label conceptualization, Carton box and handbag designing, Small box designing, and Visually appealing are the scopes of our work.

Logo Design Steps

Brand story

Stories have the power to capture any heart, so start with your’s and your product's story. The fact is that every company is built to make money. You should be able to sell yourself just as well as your product to make the business profitable. So while creating a logo there must be a story hidden in your logo. When the customers see your product name (logo), the whole picture of your brand should pop up in their minds like the color code, company, brand, images, videos, theme, and even the story behind it. A Logo should be like a name of a movie, through the color, shape, and typeface of your logo.

Brainstorm

Now It’s time to select the best word to describe your brand. For that, initially, you have to do a lot of research and should come up with some good words for your brand. Select at least 5 to 10 words that can describe what you do, why this product, and all stories behind the product like fixing a puzzle.

Sketch

Then sketch your every Idea based on the selected words on a white paper first. Keep refining until you reach a satisfying one. Try to keep the shape simple and unique to remember. Avoid using popular clip-art artwork or generic symbols which we can find anywhere because they have a tendency to get easily forgotten at first glance. Remember colors play an important role in your brand creation and logo creation. You should always have a good knowledge of the current color trend in your target market. Keep color simple and not exceed 3. Always try to use unique color combinations that stand out from your competition

Testing

The best 3 concepts are selected from the sketch and will take the opinion of others. Good research on a buyer's persona will also help you to select the best one from the sketch. This is how we will test the top sketches.

Refine

The best concept is selected, now it's time to refine the chosen sketch and perfect the story you started with.

Develop

We help you to develop your Logo's Layout on a Free Design Platform into a usable digital format to bring this design to life. Design and develop the logo with a perfect layout, text, shapes, space, and color, and alignment with the surroundings with the best tool available. So that no matter how we might repurpose your logo and where you might publish it, the logo will appear even with any surroundings.

Choose Versatile Colors and a Font

Make sure to have logo color variations to suit any background whether it’s dark or light-colored so that we only have to change the color of the font. Your logo should fit anywhere and anything. So choosing the best color and font is essential for your logo.

Scalability

Anyone should understand what the logo is. Whatever the logo size is every part of your logo should be legible. Since they are going to represent the company on multiple platforms. We have to design a logo that can be scaled down for screening onto the side of a pen.
