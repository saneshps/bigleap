Why Corporate Video?

Building brand awareness

If you want to create brand awareness and brand identity, there is no better way than a Corporate Video and that’s why they are referred to as the bare bones of any marketing technique. We suggest a company should produce both company video and corporate video. As a company video can suggest a broader overview of the whole business and corporate videos are created on the basis of their purpose and content.

Social media is the best platform you can showcase your unique corporate video to build brand awareness. While creating a full-length corporate video it is always good to create a one-minute short video highlight from that, so that you can post it is social media and it will help the viewers to get a glimpse of your business’s key areas and benefits. Short videos are more interesting to watch and people will have the patience to watch them fully.

So it is recommended to create short edits which are Social media-friendly videos of your lengthy corporate video to post on social media. And then through this short video, you have to allure them to watch your full-length corporate or business video on your website directing viewers here to see the full thing. By this, you can create more brand awareness and potential customers can learn more and hopefully buy from you.

Enhance conversion rate

Videos always capture the eyes and that’s the reason why video became the most popular medium online and can be a medium to improve business conversion rates. It is said that business videos or corporate videos on the landing page of the website can increase conversions by 80%. For this, the content of the video should be extraordinary with the best interesting content, the quality of the video, and which platform you are going to place it on. At BigLeap we provide the best team to execute the corporate video service for you.

Build trust and confidence

The main reason behind improving the conversion rate is that corporate video helps in building the trust and confidence of your target customers in your brand. Through videos, we can create a feeling in the mind of customers that this brand is a worthy and beneficial purchase. To build trust in customers rather than just showing the pictures on the website it is always good to show the videos so that people will have a feeling of they are seeing a product just like in a shop and also they can also feel the effects and benefits of that product. We can demonstrate our product or services well through the video and videos will give you a better opportunity to showcase your strengths.

Enhance the visibility

Corporate video is the best medium to boost the visibility of your products or services. Videos can be promoted in various channels effectively and among them, social media platforms are the best and cheapest way to promote your business video or corporate video. But to get high visibility there are some parameters you have to check like video quality, interesting content, information, creativity, graphic designing, etc.

The video quality should be high, the content should be of high quality, digestible and informative, and the video should be unique and creative. This will bring more likes, shares, and positive comments which will boost the visibility. YouTube. Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and many more social media platforms can help us to enhance our visibility cost-effectively. And BigLeap will make sure of that.

Search engine visibility is much more important than social media visibility. A quality corporate video can boost the SEO process since Google favors sites with good video content and often prioritizes it in results for popular search queries. It mostly takes time to rank in search engines, but if you got good quality video the it has become much easier.
