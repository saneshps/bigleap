Top Trends in Mobile application Development in 2024

There’s no doubt about the fact that mobile applications play a vital role in helping businesses build seamless customer experiences and prospects. Mobile applications and marketing strategies have grown significantly in recent years due to the fact that they can be easily adopted for new businesses and services and responsive communication. In order to acquire the rewards, the primary thing is that you should understand the latest and emerging trends in the market.

Have you ever checked how many apps you have on your mobile device? Have you thought of having a new feature daily or a larger client base for your business each hour or the numerous expectations from infinite users? All of these are a throwback of the break-neck speed evolution that’s happening in the mobile application development sector.

The mobile application development sector is constantly evolving and the evolution is happening at a break-neck speed. In order to survive and boom in this digital Darwinian epoch, you should be well familiarized with the constantly changing trends in mobile application development.

Meanwhile, another painful truth is the failure of mobile applications. The fundamental reason for the failure of mobile applications is it’s not capable of tracking as well as implementing the most modern trends. And this is just enough to lose to a competitor who is technologically sound. Constant trail and addition of some features can help you get out of this risk.

What are the top mobile application development trends in 2024?

Even Though new trends keep coming on a day today basis in the mobile application development sector, let’s check some of the top trends that we shortlisted based on our thorough research.

1. Drastic ascend of 5G

It’s been quite some time since we heard about 5G technology. But this term has become a buzzword now. The technology businesses have started to implement 5G on a larger scale and 5G enabled products are seen everywhere in today’s market. In another one year, we can expect to see almost 660 million smart devices with 5G connection which means almost close to 50 % of the devices will have 5G.

2. Effects of 5G on Mobile App Development

With the advent of 5G technology, the way mobile applications are built and used are going to change. We will see significant improvements in terms of speed and productivity.

  • In comparison to 4G, 5G is 100 times faster.
  • Response time is reduced from 50 millisecond to 1 millisecond.
  • With great resolution, response time and quick performance - applications that stream videos will benefit from great improvement.
  • 5G integration is much easier and hence it allows additional opportunities for VR and AR.
  • With the use of 5G, the transfer of data between devices will become quicker and seamless.
  • The 5G technology benefits developers by allowing them to add new features without causing any ill effect on the performance of the mobile applications.
  • Since 5G enables quicker processing of biometric data for the purpose of identification, mobile payment options tend to be faster as well as secure.

As a whole, it has been found that the 5G technology will make applications more seamless, quicker and efficient. This technology also makes a way for innovation. All the amazing creative ideas that you had in mind so far will no longer remain as imagination. You can turn it into reality with 5G.

3.Foldable Device Application

In the current smartphone market share, foldable devices remain at the peak.For sure, things will change in the future.

Unfolding your smartphone to offer a big screen is sure to have a positive impact on customers.

A big screen offers a way to a very massive and alluring experience.

Having multiple windows enables a user to process multiple tasks simultaneously.

Gaming applications and video streaming can gain maximum advantages in foldable devices just by enhancing the size of the screen or by offering more space for additional data and controls. Developing mobile applications keeping in mind the screen size will be one of the major trends in mobile app development in 2024.

4. AR and VR

Have you ever thought how the temporary success of Pokemon Go paved the way for development of AR in mobile applications? It opened the eyes of many developers to understand that VR could actually be used to offer an alluring experience to the viewers. Today, there are several brands that make use of VR and AR experience.

Shopping brands like IKEA, Loreal, Lenskart make use of VR and AR technologies to let the customers know how their products can be arranged or how it suits you.

We also see this technology being utilized by Apple, Google or META. With the help of this technology, Google offers “Live View” in Google Maps.

Another major technological advancement is LiDAR in AR. Apple refers to LiDAR as AR at the speed of light and has been used in its devices. This technology enables capturing of high quality photos even in very low light.

It’s believed that a huge spike in the amount of AR and VR users will be seen this year, meaning you get the chance to use these trends in application development and see total game changing experiences.

5. Integration in Wearable Apps

As we all know that wearable devices are preferred by everyone. According to the latest survey, there has been a drastic increase in the number of people who use wearable devices as each year passes by. This trend teaches us that the mobile app developers and companies should be ready with their products that offers superior quality digital experience to their customers who use smartwatches and other digital wearables thereby achieving a unique edge over others who do not use.

6. Food & Grocery Apps

Following the lockdown during COVID period, there has been a great boom in the use of online shopping even in the case of groceries. Even after COVID, the trend has only increased and app cateroes have been seem to be increasing at a faster pace. People have got the comfort of ordering their groceries and food from the couch and this trend seems to be only increasing day by day.

7.Gaming and Mobile Entertainment

Mobile entertainment and gaming applications have become something inevitable for a major section of the people today. This is well understood by the popularity of OTT platforms like Netflix, Amazon Prime etc. The gaming application downloads and use have also seen a significant rise in recent years and the trend is only likely to go up.

Funding, creative ideas of application, the best UX design and catering to various operating systems are very important. Privacy protection and cyber security facilities are other areas to be taken care of.
